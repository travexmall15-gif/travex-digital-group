// The Beginning

How It All Started

In 2026, in the heart of Dar es Salaam, a young Tanzanian entrepreneur named Jumanne Maregeri sat down with a single, powerful question: "Why are so many talented Tanzanian businesses invisible to the world?"

He had watched small business owners struggle daily — incredible products with no digital presence, talented entrepreneurs without tools to compete, students with business ideas but no platform to grow. The problem wasn't effort or talent. The problem was access — access to technology, to digital markets, to the tools that could amplify their potential and change their lives.

That question became an obsession. That obsession became a mission. And that mission became Travex Digital Group — Tanzania's first AI-powered digital business ecosystem, incorporated in 2026 with one unwavering belief: technology should solve real problems for real people, not just the privileged few.

What started as a single marketplace — Travex Mall — quickly evolved into something far bigger. Travex Campus brought student entrepreneurship to life across 5 major universities. Travex Social Vybe created Africa's first business social network. And with Travex Stay and Travex Move on the horizon, the group is building Tanzania's most complete digital ecosystem — one platform, one community, one transformation at a time.

"I didn't build Travex to create a tech company. I built it to be a solution — a bridge between Tanzania's incredible human potential and the intelligent digital tools they deserve to succeed."
— Jumanne Maregeri, Founder & CEO, Travex Digital Group
