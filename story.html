Our Story

Built Out of Frustration.
Driven by Purpose.

Before you read the numbers, understand what QNEX360 is at its core. It was not born out of a business school case study or a venture capital thesis. It was born out of frustration, the same frustration that millions of Tanzanian business owners feel every single day.

Watch a shopkeeper write an order in a paper notebook. Watch a customer ask for M-Pesa payment details over a WhatsApp call. Watch a delivery rider being recruited through word of mouth because there is no platform to connect them. This is the daily reality for millions of Tanzanian businesses, and it is entirely unnecessary.

QNEX360 is the answer to that reality. A digital operating system built specifically for African SMEs, not adapted from a Western product, not translated from English, but designed from the ground up for how Tanzanian business actually works.

We are a company built by a student, during his studies, using only personal resources and determination. ShopNekt and QNEX360 Finance are live today. They work. They are production-grade. They were built while everything said it could not be done.

Two live products. Six founders. One city. One mission: to prove that Africa's next great digital companies will be built by Africans, for Africans, and compete with anyone globally.

Mission

Give every East African SME the ability to sell, manage money, and move goods, from one platform, in Kiswahili, on a smartphone.

Vision

To become the foundational digital commerce infrastructure for over 8 million SMEs across the East African Community.

Jumanne Maregeri
Founder and CEO, QNEX360
